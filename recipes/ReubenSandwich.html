Reuben Sandwich

Description

A Reuben is a grilled sandwich featuring corned beef on rye bread. The Reuben is a menu staple in Jewish-style delicatessens, but it's not technically kosher because it combines meat and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices rye bread
  • Â½ cup Thousand Island dressing
  • 8 slices Swiss cheese
  • 8 slices deli sliced corned beef
  • 1 cup sauerkraut, drained
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened

How to make

  • Spread the dressing on one side of each slice of bread.
  • Assemble the sandwiches. Butter the top of each sandwich.
  • Place the sandwich butter-side down on the hot skillet or griddle.
  • Butter the other side. Flip when the bottom slice is golden brown.
  • Grill until both sides are golden brown.
